What Changed

Removed topic pills — the algorithm auto-focuses on your weakest areas. As you improve in one topic, it shifts to the next weakest.

Removed algorithm breakdown — users don't need to know the 60/20/20 split. They just trust us.

How Topic Focus Works (Internal)

The smart algorithm already handles this automatically:

1. 6 of 10 questions come from the user's weakest subtopics (by accuracy)

2. 2 questions from developing areas (50-80% accuracy)

3. 2 questions from strong areas (reinforcement)

4. As the user masters a weak topic, the next weakest one naturally takes its place in the 60% bucket

5. Novel/never-attempted topics get a 1.5x weight bonus, so they surface early

None of this is exposed to the user — they just see "Start Practice" and get good questions.
