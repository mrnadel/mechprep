Duolingo-Style Design Elements

Stats strip — divided cells in one card, no separate boxes

What The User Sees vs What Happens

User sees: "We picked the perfect questions for you" + big START button

What happens: Smart algorithm selects 6 weak + 2 medium + 2 strong questions, adapted to user's level, avoiding recently-answered ones

After session: History cards show which topics were covered — so users learn what they practiced, not what they'll practice
