Design Decisions

One big CTA — tap "Start Practice" and go. No mode selection, no friction.

Optional topic filter — scrollable pills above the button. Default is "All Topics" (smart algorithm picks the mix). Tap a topic to focus.

Smart algorithm breakdown — shows the user why their questions are personalized (60/20/20 split). Builds trust.

Stats row — accuracy, total practiced, weak area count. Minimal but motivating.
