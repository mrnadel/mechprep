Where should the lesson background appear?

The background (animated SVGs, space scene, etc.) needs a layer to live on. Which approach fits best?

A. Full-Screen Background

Background fills the entire lesson screen. Header, question cards, and buttons float on top with glass/transparent styling. Most immersive.

B. Question Zone Only

Background appears only behind the question/content area. Header and footer stay normal. Less immersive, simpler to implement.

C. Subtle Accent Layer

Background elements (stars, planets) peek around the edges of normal solid cards. Cards stay opaque. Atmospheric without changing readability.
