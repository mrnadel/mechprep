Ten units. One complete preparation.

Every subject area that appears in mechanical engineering interviews, organized into focused, manageable units.

01

Statics

Free-body diagrams, equilibrium, trusses, friction, centroids and moments of inertia.

02

Dynamics

Kinematics, Newton's laws, energy methods, impulse-momentum, rigid body dynamics.

03

Strength of Materials

Stress, strain, beam bending, torsion, combined loading, Mohr's circle, buckling.

04

Thermodynamics

Laws of thermodynamics, cycles, entropy, properties of substances, ideal gases.

05

Heat Transfer

Conduction, convection, radiation, fins, heat exchangers, transient analysis.

06

Fluid Mechanics

Hydrostatics, Bernoulli, viscous flow, pipe systems, dimensional analysis.

07

Materials Science

Crystal structures, phase diagrams, mechanical properties, failure analysis, selection.

08

Machine Elements

Bearings, gears, fasteners, springs, shaft design, fatigue life prediction.

09

GD&T

Geometric dimensioning and tolerancing, datums, feature control frames, tolerance stacks.

10

Interview Prep

Behavioral questions, problem-solving frameworks, case studies, communication strategies.
