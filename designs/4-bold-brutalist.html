The Curriculum

10 Units. Zero Gaps.

Every topic a mechanical engineering interviewer might throw at you, broken into focused, learnable units.

01

Statics

Free body diagrams, equilibrium, trusses, friction, centroids and moments of inertia.

02

Dynamics

Kinematics, Newton's laws, energy methods, impulse-momentum, rigid body motion.

03

Strength of Materials

Stress, strain, Mohr's circle, beam deflection, torsion, column buckling.

04

Thermodynamics

Laws of thermodynamics, cycles, entropy, ideal gas, phase diagrams, availability.

05

Heat Transfer

Conduction, convection, radiation, fins, heat exchangers, transient analysis.

06

Fluid Mechanics

Bernoulli, Navier-Stokes, pipe flow, boundary layers, drag, dimensional analysis.

07

Materials Science

Crystal structures, phase diagrams, hardening mechanisms, composites, failure modes.

08

Machine Elements

Gears, bearings, shafts, fasteners, springs, welded joints, fatigue design.

09

GD&T

Datums, tolerances, true position, MMC/LMC, profile, runout, flatness.

10

Interview Prep

Behavioral questions, system design, estimation problems, whiteboard problem-solving.
