Duolingo for Professions

Expanding from MechReady into a multi-profession gamified learning platform. Which professions have the biggest untapped markets?

The Big Picture

The gamified learning market is exploding. Duolingo proved the model at $1B+ revenue — and they've expanded into math, music, and chess. But they have NOT entered professional education. No profession today has a Duolingo-quality gamified mobile experience for exam prep. Every market relies on textbooks, video lectures, and static practice questions.

Tier 3 — Avoid for Now

Bottom Line

Skilled Trades is the single biggest opportunity — 680K+ apprentices growing to 1M, massive worker shortage, $145M government investment, and virtually zero gamified competition. Combined with insurance licensing (383K exam takers, 58% pass rate) and your existing engineering content, a multi-profession platform could address millions of annual learners across professions that are currently stuck with textbooks and static video courses.

The gamification gap is real. Duolingo hasn't entered professional education. AMBOSS proved healthcare education can reach $863M valuation. The first to build a true Duolingo-quality experience across professional certifications will own the category.
